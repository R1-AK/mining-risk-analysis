Risk Impact Analysis of Mining Activities

Choose your analysis method to assess environmental and socioeconomic risks.

Spatial Analysis

Analyze risks within concentric buffer zones (e.g., 1, 5, 25 km) around mining sites. Ideal for assessing proximity-based impacts on static datasets like Protected Areas, Forest Cover, and Human Footprint.

Flowpath Analysis

Model how potential contaminants (e.g., tailings) travel downstream from mining sites. Uses a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) to simulate flow over terrain, assessing impacts on Population, Agriculture, and Forests along the path.
